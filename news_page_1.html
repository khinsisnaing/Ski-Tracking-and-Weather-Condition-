2 BILLION WATCHED BEIJING 2022 WINTER OLYMPICS

20TH OCTOBER 2022

Viewers around the world watched a combined 713 billion minutes of Games coverage on Olympic Media Rights Partners’ channels – an 18% increase compared with PyeongChang 2018.


The IOC said that it underlined “the continued appetite for Olympic broadcast coverage in an increasingly fragmented media landscape”.


Media Rights Partners made more hours of coverage available to people than at any previous Olympic Winter Games, including a record 120,670 hours through digital platforms.


Other findings include that almost half of the Beijing 2022 viewers surveyed said they wanted to watch more coverage of the Olympic Games in the future.
